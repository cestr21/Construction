About Us

Quality Craftsmanship, Personalized Service

Who We Are

SJV Construction is a locally owned business specializing in finished carpentry, woodworking, and general remodeling. With over 10 years of combined experience, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail in every project we undertake. Whether it's custom trim work, intricate woodwork, or remodeling, our team ensures the highest standards of quality.

Our Expertise

We are experts in custom carpentry and woodworking projects, offering solutions that range from small residential jobs to large-scale remodeling projects. No matter the size, our experienced crew is dedicated to making your vision a reality with precision and care. We approach each project with a commitment to quality and a focus on meeting the specific needs of our clients.

Our Mission

At SJV Construction, our mission is simple: to provide superior construction services with integrity and professionalism. We believe in building long-lasting relationships with our clients through honest communication and outstanding results. Whether you're in need of custom woodwork or a full home remodel, SJV Construction is here to bring your ideas to life.
